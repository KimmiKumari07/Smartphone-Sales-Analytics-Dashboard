# 🚀 Smartphone Sales Analytics Dashboard

## 📊 Overview
The **Smartphone Sales Analytics Dashboard** is a Power BI project designed to analyze smartphone sales data across multiple dimensions such as brands, regions, and customer demographics.

It delivers actionable insights through interactive visualizations, enabling data-driven decision-making for sales and marketing strategies.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall smartphone sales performance  
- Identify top-performing brands and regions  
- Understand customer purchasing behavior  
- Provide clear and interactive business insights  

---

## 📌 Key Features

### 🔢 KPIs (Key Performance Indicators)
- Total Transactions  
- Units Sold  
- Total Revenue  
- Average Selling Price  

### 📈 Sales Analysis
- Sales by **Brand**  
- Sales by **Country/Region**  
- Sales by **Customer Demographics** (Age Group, Gender, etc.)  

### 📊 Visual Insights
- Time-series trend analysis  
- Brand performance comparison  
- Regional sales distribution  
- Customer segmentation insights  

---

## 🏗️ Architecture

```
Excel Dataset 
   ↓
Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
   ↓
Power BI Data Model (Relationships)
   ↓
DAX Measures (KPIs & Calculations)
   ↓
Dashboard Visuals (Charts & Graphs)
   ↓
Business Insights
```
